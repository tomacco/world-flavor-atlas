Navigating the Geometry of Food

You're looking at 250 food ingredients from cuisines around the world, mapped into 3D space by how they relate to each other. Ingredients that appear together in recipes cluster together. Ingredients that share flavor chemistry pull closer.

Three embedding models — COOC (co-occurrence in recipes), CORE (blended), and CHEM (flavor chemistry) — each organize the same ingredients differently. Drag the slider to watch the clusters reshape in real time.

Based on the paper by Radzikowski & Chen (2026), trained on 4.14M recipes across 7 languages.
